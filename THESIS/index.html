ABOUT US

Our Story

At Chapter One, we’ve created a cozy haven for coffee lovers and book enthusiasts alike. Nestled in a warm and inviting space, our café is the perfect place to unwind, enjoy expertly crafted drinks, and savor delicious treats.
Every two weeks, we host a special Book Club gathering — a chance to connect, share your love for reading, and dive into engaging discussions about carefully selected books.
Whether you’re a casual reader or a passionate bibliophile, everyone is welcome. To join the Book Club, please fill out the registration form available on our website.
This helps us prepare a welcoming space for all attendees. Come for the coffee, stay for the stories. Your next chapter starts here, at Chapter One.

THE LIST

Menu list

At Chapter One, we craft each cup with care — from the rich, velvety Matcha and classic Latte to the indulgent Mocha and perfectly brewed Filter Coffee. Craving something traditional? Enjoy our authentic Turkish Tea and aromatic Turkish Coffee, prepared just the way you like it.
Pair your drink with our delightful homemade treats, moist Carrot Cake or creamy Cheesecake. The perfect companions to your coffee break. Whether you're catching up with friends, working quietly, or simply taking a moment for yourself, Chapter One is your welcoming space to relax and recharge.
